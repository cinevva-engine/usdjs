## Code of Conduct

We follow the **Contributor Covenant**.

### Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

### Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- being respectful and inclusive
- focusing on technical merit and user impact
- giving and receiving feedback constructively

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- trolling, personal attacks, or sustained disruption

### Enforcement

Report issues to `security@cinevva.com`.

We may remove, edit, or reject contributions and ban contributors for behavior we deem inappropriate.
